# Retail-Insights-Advanced-Business-Analysis

## Project Overview

A extensive business intelligence analysis of a retail business in Australia that covered 5000 orders, 789 customers and 257 products across New South Wales and Victoria from 2013 to 2017.

This Project shows how to use sophisticated Excel technique for end-to-end data analysis, from executive dashboards, CLV Tiers and Account Manager performance monitoring to raw data cleaning.

---

## Business Problem

The business needed answers to five critical questions:

1. Which products drive the most profit and which are dead weight?
2. Which customers are most valuable and at risk of churning?
3. When does demand peak and how should inventory be planned?
4. Which account managers are performing and which need support?
5. Are we actually delivering on our service promises to our customer?

## Key Findings

### 1. Product Profitability  
-**21 products (8% of SKUs) generates 80% of net profit** 
- Top product: Cando PC940 copier at $412,128 net profit
- 188 products generates only 5% of profit combined
- Recommendation: Concentrate marketing and stock on 21 products only

### 2. Customer Lifetime Value
-**Top 10% of customers (78 people) drive 55.6% of all profit**
- Champion tier customers average $15,422 lifetime profit each
- Recommendation: Dedicated VIP retention program for top 78 accounts

### 3. Seasonality 
-**October is peak month - demand runs 26% above annual average**
- June is the weakest month at 22% below average
- Recommendation: Build 30% extra inventory before October each year

### 4. Account Manager Performance
- Connor Betts leads $383,349 profit across 892 orders
- Composite KPI scoring (Revenue 30%, Profit 50%, Average order value 20%) identifying 7 AMs needing support
- 5 account manager name typos found and corrected in source data

## 🔧 Excel Techniques Used
 
**Formulas**
- SUMIFS, COUNTIFS, AVERAGEIFS — conditional aggregation
- XLOOKUP, INDEX/MATCH — dynamic lookups
- SUMPRODUCT — conditional COGS calculation
- IF, IFS, IFERROR — logical and error handling
 
**Analysis Frameworks**
- ABC Product Classification (Pareto/cumulative profit method)
- Customer Lifetime Value tiering (Champion/Loyal/Growing/New)
- Monthly Seasonality Index calculation
- Weighted Composite KPI Scoring (min-max normalization)
- Profit Waterfall (Gross Revenue to Net Profit)
